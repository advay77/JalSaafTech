Product Description

The IoT Water Quality Monitoring System is a smart device that continuously tracks water parameters like pH, turbidity, and temperature. Using real-time sensors, it delivers accurate data to a connected app or cloud platform for easy monitoring. This system ensures safe water quality for drinking, agriculture, and industrial use. With customizable alerts, it notifies users of any abnormalities instantly. Its compact, energy-efficient design makes it ideal for both residential and commercial applications.
